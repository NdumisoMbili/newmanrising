PROGRAMS

Ignite The Mind.

Five transformative programs designed to build character, sharpen minds, and open doors. Real skills. Real brotherhood. Real futures.

What We Offer

Five Pillars of Transformation

Each program is a deliberate investment in the next generation — equipping young men with the voice, discipline, financial wisdom, and global opportunity they deserve.

01 — Expression

Speakers' Club

Every young man has a voice — we teach him how to use it with power and grace. The Speakers' Club is a comprehensive expression program that goes far beyond public speaking.

✓Competitive debates in schools — sharpening logic and argumentation
✓Storytelling & poetry — finding beauty and power in one's own narrative
✓Theatre & drama — building confidence, empathy, and stage presence
✓Reading clubs — books and novels that expand the mind and the world
✓Spoken language — accents, dialects, and multilingual communication
Debates Storytelling Poetry Theatre Reading Languages
02
menu_book
02 — Brotherhood

Homework Club

Brotherhood begins at home, and academic excellence is the foundation of a rising man's future. The Homework Club redefines how young men approach their studies — together.

✓Parent coaching — teaching parents how to create a structured homework environment at home
✓Boys taking ownership — peer-led homework sessions where older students guide younger ones
✓Collaborative learning — group problem-solving skills and teamwork that sharpen young minds
✓Brotherhood bonds — building lasting friendships through shared academic commitment
Academic Excellence Parent Engagement Peer Learning Teamwork

sports
03 — Discipline

Sports

Sport is not just a game — it is a forge. It builds discipline, focus, resilience, and character. We use sport as a tool to keep young men's minds sharp, their bodies strong, and their hearts far from the streets, drugs, and alcohol.

✓Boxing — discipline, self-control, and resilience training
✓Football, rugby, and team sports — unity and leadership
✓Cycling, golf, tennis — individual mastery and focus
✓Keeping minds away from streets, drugs, and alcohol
Boxing Football Rugby Cycling Golf Tennis

account_balance
04 — Wealth

Financial Literacy

In partnership with African Bank, New Man Rising travels to schools across provinces to equip students from 18 years old with the financial knowledge and tools they need to thrive. This program is life-changing — and it opens the door to work and income.

✓School visits across provinces — gathering students 18+ to open real bank accounts
✓African Bank funds travel, catering, and accommodation for provincial trips
✓Venue hire for structured financial literacy workshops and presentations
✓Students hired to work in NMR projects — their accounts become active with real income
Banking Financial Skills Savings Employment Provincial Reach African Bank
05
travel_explore
05 — Global

SA Scouts

In partnership with SA Scouts, we identify talented young men from the streets and townships and place them into the Scouts program where they develop world-class skills. The reward? An international future.

✓NMR recruits youth from communities and places them with SA Scouts for skills training
✓Once graduates turn 18, SA Scouts handles their paperwork for overseas placement
✓Scouts fund NMR to continue recruiting more youth from South African communities
✓A pipeline from township streets to international employment opportunities
Life Skills Overseas Placement Global Careers SA Scouts Community Recruitment
